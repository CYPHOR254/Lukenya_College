Our students, travelling from across Kenya, normally join us after completing their KCSE. We offer them a unique opportunity: to train as teachers or acquire business or train in other technical areas within the exciting context of real-life industry experiences. My goal as a Director is to ensure that their individual and collective success is at the heart of every action and decision made at the Lukenya College. We guide them as they lay the foundations for what will be the start of an exciting educational experience that leads to outstanding achievements in the fields of Engineering and Science.

Graduates of Lukenya College are well rounded, confident and articulate. They are also academically, technically and practically experienced in their areas as they graduate from this college. They are always ready and determined to work in the industry and by extension prepared for a lifelong journey of learning throughout their working careers.

Why choose us

Broad based education for a better Kenya

To be a dynamic college, committed to scholarship in teaching, research, training and community service.

Vision

To be a dynamic college, committed to scholarship in teaching, research, training and community service.

Mission

To provide demand driven curricula and adequate resources with the aim of producing graduates equipped with skills relevant to market needs.

Philosophy

To Embrace a Green philosophy which fosters the use of innovative technologies in global food security, energy and sustainability, health and universal education.
